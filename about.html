ABOUT ME

I’m a graphic designer, photographer, and caffeine lover currently living in Toronto. I’m currently a full time student in the joint York University / Sheridan College Bachelor of Design program. I've loved analysing and creating art and design since I was little. I adore creating designs that are clean, functional, and accessible and my work reflects that. I believe that when the tools and objects that we interact with are designed well, the overall quality of life in our society will be improved. When I'm not creating things you'll probably find me either running or reading a book with a fresh cup of tea, earl gray, hot.
